Subscription process

Soon after you have pressed the 'Subscribe' button, you will receive an email or letter asking you to confirm your email and/or postal address. Once you have done that, you will receive the current issue of the Farming Futures newsletter within a few days. You will subsequently receive new issues on a regular basis. Our newsletter is currently published monthly.

How to unsubscribe

You can cancel your email subscription at any time by pressing the button provided on your newsletter email. To unsubscribe from a postal-only subscription, please call +353 1 000 0000 or send an email with your subscription number on the subject line to unsubscribe@farmingfutures.ie.
